Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Rainbow.

How much does Rainbow cost? ▼

Rainbow offers flexible pricing plans to suit teams of all sizes. You can start with a free trial to explore the platform before committing to a paid plan. Contact us for detailed pricing tailored to your organisation.

Is Rainbow secure and compliant? ▼

Yes. Rainbow is built with enterprise-grade security and is hosted on sovereign cloud infrastructure. It meets rigorous compliance standards including GDPR and ANSSI certification requirements.

Does Rainbow integrate with Microsoft Teams? ▼

Yes, Rainbow integrates seamlessly with Microsoft Teams and Outlook, allowing you to bring telephony and collaboration features into your existing workflow.

How long does deployment take? ▼

Rainbow can be deployed quickly — often within a few days. The cloud-based architecture means no complex on-premise setup. Your team can be up and running with minimal IT overhead.

Can I use Rainbow with my existing phone system? ▼

Yes, Rainbow is designed to work with existing telephony infrastructure including PBX systems, SIP trunks and analogue lines, making migration straightforward.

What devices is Rainbow available on? ▼

Rainbow is available on desktop (Windows, Mac, Linux), mobile (iOS, Android) and via web browser — so your team can stay connected from any device.

Is there support available for deployment? ▼

Absolutely. Professional services include deployment assistance, team training and ongoing technical support to ensure a smooth rollout and adoption.
